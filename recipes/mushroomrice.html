Easy Mushroom Rice

Description

Make scrumptious mushroom rice casserole dish- it's as simple as can be!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup incooked long-grain rice
  • 1 (10.5) can condensed french onion soup
  • 1 (10.5) can beef broth
  • 1 (4 ounce) can sliced mushrooms,drained
  • 1/4 cup butter

Steps:

    Step 1

  1. Gather the ingredients and preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degress C).

    2. Step 2

  2. Combine rice,condensed soup,beef broth,mushroom,and butter in 8-incj square casserole dish.

    3. Step 3

  3. Cover and bake in the preheated oven for 60 minutes.
